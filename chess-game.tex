\subsection*{How to play}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Look at the move history above to see the engine's reply.
  \item Add your next move as \texttt{\textbackslash playmove\{e2e4\}} (coordinate notation: from-square to-square).
  \item Recompile. The engine picks a random legal move and responds.
  \item Repeat until checkmate or draw!
\end{enumerate}

\noindent\textit{Examples: \texttt{e2e4} (pawn), \texttt{g1f3} (knight), \texttt{e1g1} (kingside castle).}

\medskip\noindent\textit{Engine responses are stable across recompiles. Change
\texttt{\textbackslash gameseed\{...\}} to start a different game.
Set \texttt{\textbackslash enginestrength} to 0 (random), 1 (greedy), 2 (depth-2), or 3 (depth-3 + quiescence, default).}
